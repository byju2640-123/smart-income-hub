## Link Anchor Text Best Practices

✅ **Good Anchor Text:**
- Descriptive and relevant
- Contains keywords naturally
- Matches link destination
- Examples: "AI tools for productivity," "affiliate marketing," "Pinterest strategies"

❌ **Poor Anchor Text:**
- Generic: "click here," "read more," "learn more"
- Keyword stuffed: "best free AI tools for beginners that work in 2026"
- Misleading: Link text doesn't match destination
